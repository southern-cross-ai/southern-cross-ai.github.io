Welcome to Team ModelWorks

We are assembling a team of 7 innovative and skilled individuals to develop impactful, real-world AI solutions. This is your chance to contribute to applications that solve real-world problems and make a tangible difference across industries.

About ModelWorks

ModelWorks is focused on creating advanced AI solutions tailored to solve practical problems. Our goal is to build systems that are efficient, scalable, and impactful across various sectors, from healthcare to finance and beyond.

The Talent We Need

We are looking for students with expertise in:

  • AI Development and Engineering
  • Software Architecture and Design
  • Data Analysis and Visualization
  • System Optimization and Scalability
  • Domain Knowledge in Applied AI (e.g., healthcare, robotics, finance)

Your Role

As a member of Team ModelWorks, you will:

  • Design, develop, and implement AI-powered applications
  • Collaborate with domain experts to ensure solutions meet real-world needs
  • Optimize systems for performance and scalability
  • Contribute to cutting-edge AI research and development
  • Share your findings and progress with the team
