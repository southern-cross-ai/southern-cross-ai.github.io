Welcome to Team JoeyLLM

We are assembling a team of 7 passionate and skilled individuals to shape Australia's next-generation language model. This is an exciting opportunity to work at the cutting edge of AI technology, contributing to projects that will revolutionize education, business, and innovation.

About JoeyLLM

JoeyLLM is focused on building an advanced language model tailored to the unique needs of Australia. Our mission is to create a system that excels in understanding and generating human language, driving breakthroughs in AI-driven communication and automation.

The Talent We Need

We are looking for students with expertise in:

  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Machine Learning and Deep Learning
  • Data Engineering and Large-scale Data Management
  • Model Optimization and Deployment
  • AI Ethics and Fairness

Your Role

As a member of Team JoeyLLM, you will:

  • Contribute to designing, training, and testing the language model
  • Collaborate with team members to solve complex technical challenges
  • Explore and implement cutting-edge AI techniques
  • Present your work in meetings and share insights with the team
