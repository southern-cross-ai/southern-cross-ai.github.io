Welcome to Team AmplifiAI

We are assembling a team of 7 creative and skilled individuals to revolutionize AI outreach and engagement. Join us in enhancing awareness and accessibility of AI through innovative multimedia and marketing strategies.

About AmplifiAI

AmplifiAI focuses on creating and delivering impactful multimedia content and campaigns that promote AI's potential and accessibility. Our mission is to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI technologies and the wider community through creativity and communication.

The Talent We Need

We are looking for students with expertise in:

  • Graphic Design and Multimedia Production
  • Social Media Strategy and Management
  • Content Creation (Blogs, Videos, Podcasts)
  • Marketing and Public Relations
  • Community Engagement and Event Planning

Your Role

As a member of Team AmplifiAI, you will:

  • Develop and execute creative marketing campaigns
  • Create multimedia content to promote AI initiatives
  • Engage with the community through events and online platforms
  • Collaborate with team members to craft compelling AI narratives
  • Analyze campaign performance and optimize for impact
