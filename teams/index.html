About Us

Southern Cross AI is an open-source community bringing together AI specialists, hobbyists, academics, and enthusiasts across Australia. Our mission is to foster innovation, collaboration, and education in artificial intelligence.

Key initiatives include:

  • Developing Joey, a large language model crafted in Australia.
  • Engaging with businesses, governments, and researchers to shape AI's future.
  • Hosting monthly meetups to share knowledge and insights.
